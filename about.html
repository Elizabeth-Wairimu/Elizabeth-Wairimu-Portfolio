From Maps to Marketing

My unique journey began in cartography and GIS analysis, where I developed a keen eye for data patterns and spatial relationships. This analytical foundation became the cornerstone of my approach to digital marketing.

I believe that successful marketing isn't just about creativity it's about understanding your audience through data. Every campaign I design is backed by thorough research, strategic planning, and continuous optimization.

My GIS background gives me a unique perspective on audience segmentation and geographic targeting that most marketers lack, allowing me to deliver highly targeted campaigns with exceptional ROI.
